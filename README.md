When you press the big red [+] widget of the homepage, a new roadmap is created. You can modify it by **right clicking** on its card.

Once your roadmap is ready, **left click** on it and start creating your personal roadmap!

Press the big [+] widget to add a new milestone called "Site". Then again, you can modify it by **right clicking** on its card. **Left clicking** a Site card will mark it as completed.
